Common Questions

How is PulseFlow different from PulseCommand?

PulseFlow is the growth tier -- 60 social posts, 5 landing pages, email campaigns, and blog articles. PulseCommand adds AI avatar videos, podcast distribution on 4 platforms, custom domain, YouTube channel management, and white-glove onboarding with unlimited landing pages and 150+ posts.

Do I need to write any of the content myself?

No. AI writes everything based on your answers about your business. You review and approve -- or we handle posting too.

What is a HeyGen AI avatar?

HeyGen creates a photorealistic digital version of you that can deliver scripted video content. It looks like you recorded a professional video -- without ever sitting in front of a camera. This feature is exclusive to PulseCommand.

What does white-glove onboarding mean?

A real person from our team handles every setup step -- voice clone, HeyGen avatar, domain configuration, YouTube channel, podcast distribution. You don't set up anything yourself. Most setups are complete within 5 business days.

Is there a contract?

No. Both PulseFlow and PulseCommand are month-to-month. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.

How does the $39 first-month deal work?

New Apex representatives get their first month of PulseFlow for just $39 -- a $110 discount. After the first month, it continues at the regular member rate of $129/mo.
